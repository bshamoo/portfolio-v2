PROJECT 001

EUCALC

2020

A Chrome Extension Calculator.

Web Development

Perform arithmetic, conversions, and Euclidean inspired math in the comfort of your Google Chrome browser.


EUCALC is a calculator in the form of a Google Chrome Extension that provides users the ability to evaluate equations and basic logic, calculate conversions, and perform integer property functions.


Designed with the Google color scheme in mind, EUCALC was developed for the need to quickly calculate and verify equations and the want to develop my React.js skills.
